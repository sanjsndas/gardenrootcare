1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how GardenRoot Care ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or use our services. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. Our privacy practices comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We regularly review and update our privacy practices to ensure they meet the highest standards of data protection and privacy.

This policy applies to information we collect on this website, in email, text, and other electronic communications between you and this website, and through mobile and desktop applications you download from this website. It does not apply to information collected by us offline or through any other means, including on any other website operated by any third party.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our website, including personal information that you provide when filling out forms on our website, registering for an account, subscribing to our service, posting material, or requesting further services. This may include your name, postal address, email address, telephone number, payment information, and any other identifier by which you may be contacted online or offline.

We also collect information about your internet connection, the equipment you use to access our website, and usage details. This includes traffic data, location data, logs, and other communication data and the resources that you access and use on the website. As you navigate through and interact with our website, we may use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide services, communicate with you, and improve our offerings. We may also use your information to send you promotional materials, newsletters, or other information that may be of interest to you. The legal basis for processing your personal data may include your consent, the performance of a contract, compliance with a legal obligation, or our legitimate business interests.

We will never use your personal information for purposes that are incompatible with those described in this Privacy Policy without first obtaining your explicit consent. We may also use your information to improve our website, products, and services, and to personalize your experience by presenting content and offers tailored to your interests.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We regularly review our security practices to ensure the safety of your data. Our security measures include encryption of sensitive data, secure server configurations, regular security audits, and employee training on data protection best practices.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to take steps to protect your personal information, such as using strong passwords and keeping your login credentials confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may also have the right to restrict or object to certain processing activities, the right to data portability, and the right not to be subject to automated decision-making.

We will respond to all legitimate requests within one month, though this period may be extended for complex requests. There is typically no charge for exercising your rights, unless your request is manifestly unfounded or excessive. We may ask you to verify your identity before responding to such requests to ensure the security of your personal information.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve our services. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. The types of cookies we use include strictly necessary cookies (required for website operation), performance cookies (to analyze website usage), functionality cookies (to remember your preferences), and targeting cookies (to deliver relevant advertising).

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that some website features may not function properly without them. We also use cookies to track user activity on our website, which helps us understand how visitors interact with our content and improve our services accordingly.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to help us operate our website and provide our services. These third parties may have access to your personal information, but they are required to protect it and use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed. Our third-party service providers include web hosting companies, payment processors, email service providers, and analytics platforms.

We carefully vet all third-party providers to ensure they comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate security measures. We only share the minimum amount of personal information necessary for these providers to perform their services, and we have agreements in place to ensure that your data is handled securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
